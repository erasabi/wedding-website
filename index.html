Events

Saturday 25th May | Welcome Party 9:30PM

Can't wait till Sunday to see everyone? Then come to our Welcome Party located in the main villa of the wedding venue! There will be music, an endless open bar, but no food so make sure to have dinner before, okay?

Sunday 26th May | Ceremony 5:00PM

The wedding ceremony will be outdoors and begin promptly near the gazebo behind the main villa. It won't be all that long either, so make sure to come on time or risk missing the oppurtunity to see the wedding of two really good looking people, who didn't settle for each other, start a marriage that is actually going to last.

Sunday 26th May | Cocktail Hour 6:00PM

Open bar is the best, am I right? Anything less at a wedding is just tacky in my opinion. Well as great as it is, our cocktail hour is more like a cocktail half-hour because we're operating on a tight schedule if we want to maximize memories today. So make the most of your time, British side of my family.

Sunday 26th May | Reception 7:00PM

Reception. That's just a fancy word for party time babbbyyyy! The reception will take place in the event space adjacent to the ceremony. So if you are having a hard time finding it, let me know so I can pray for you.

Sunday 26th May | Official Afterparty 11:00PM-2AM

For those of you who feel you got a lot left in the tank come 11PM. Don't worry! We're just moving it into the villa, where our DJ will continue to party till 2AM. If you think you might want to stick around for the afterpart then bring a bathing suit and towel for swimming as well as a fresh change of clothes so you're not stuck in your sweaty formalwear.
